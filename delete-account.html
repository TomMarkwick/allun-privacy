We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to permanently delete your Allun account.

How to Delete Your Account

In-App Deletion (Recommended)

1. Open the Allun app

2. Go to Settings → Account

3. Tap "Delete My Account"

4. Read the warning and confirm deletion

5. Your account will be permanently deleted

Email Deletion Request

If you can't access the app, email us at:

support@allunapp.co.uk

Include in your email:

  • Your account email address
  • Request: "Please delete my account and all associated data"

We'll process your request within 30 days.

Warning: This action is irreversible. All your data will be permanently deleted. Please ensure you've downloaded any records you need before proceeding.

What Gets Deleted

  • Email address and login credentials
  • Date of birth
  • All lottery entries
  • Payment history
  • Push notification tokens
  • Game progress and winnings

Data Retention & Privacy

  • Your account is deleted immediately when you confirm
  • Anonymized transaction records may be retained for up to 90 days for legal and accounting compliance
  • These records cannot be linked back to your identity
  • After 90 days, all traces of your account are completely removed from our systems
