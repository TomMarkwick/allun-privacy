Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Sabian Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use the Allun daily prize draw mobile application ("the App"). Please read it carefully. By creating an account or using the App you confirm that you have read and understood this policy.

1. Who We Are

The App is operated by Sabian Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales. Sabian Ltd is the data controller responsible for the personal information described in this policy.

Contact us:
Sabian Ltd
Email: tom@allunapp.co.uk

If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us at the address above before raising a formal complaint.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the App:

  • Email address – used to identify your account and send service communications.
  • Password hash – a one-way cryptographic hash of your chosen password. We never store your plain-text password.
  • Date of birth – used solely to verify that you meet the minimum age requirement of 18 years.
  • Draw entries – a record of the daily draw entries associated with your account.
  • Push notification token – provided by your device operating system and used to deliver draw results and other service notifications via Expo's push notification service.
  • Apple or Google account identifier – if you choose to sign in using Apple or Google, we receive a unique, non-reversible identifier (the provider's "subject" ID) to link your Allun account to that sign-in. We do not receive your Apple or Google password.
  • Display name (optional, Apple only) – when you first sign in with Apple you may choose whether to share your real name or a private name. If shared, Apple provides it to us only on that first sign-in and we store it as your Allun display name.
  • Payment information Stripe only – if you make a purchase, payment card details are entered directly into Stripe's secure interface. We do not receive, process, or store any card numbers, CVV codes, or bank account details. See Section 4.

We do not collect your phone number, postal address, or any browsing or behavioural data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used solely to operate the Allun daily prize draw. Specifically:

  • Authenticating you when you sign in.
  • Verifying you are 18 or older before permitting participation.
  • Recording and validating your draw entries.
  • Delivering push notifications about draw results, account activity, and service updates.
  • Complying with our legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the operation of the draw.

The legal bases we rely on under UK GDPR are: performance of a contract (operating the draw for you) and compliance with a legal obligation (age verification and anti-money-laundering requirements where applicable).

4. Payment Processing

All payment transactions are handled exclusively by Stripe, Inc., a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. When you enter payment details in the App you are interacting directly with Stripe's secure, encrypted interface.

We do not receive, store, log, or have access to your full card number, card verification code, or any other sensitive payment credential. The only payment-related information we hold is a Stripe customer ID and a record of whether a transaction was successful.

Stripe's own privacy policy governs the processing of your payment data and is available at stripe.com/gb/privacy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share it only with the following sub-processors, and only to the extent necessary to operate the App:

  • Stripe, Inc. – payment processing (see Section 4).
  • Supabase, Inc. – cloud database and authentication infrastructure that stores your account data securely on our behalf.
  • Expo (Expo, Inc.) – mobile application platform used to deliver push notifications to your device.
  • Apple Inc. – identity verification for Sign in with Apple. Apple receives only the information required to authenticate the sign-in.
  • Google LLC – identity verification for Sign in with Google. Google receives only the information required to authenticate the sign-in.
  • Resend, Inc. – transactional email delivery (account confirmations, draw results, and service notices). Resend processes your email address and the content of service messages on our behalf.

Each sub-processor is contractually required to process your data only for the purpose of providing their service to us and in accordance with applicable data protection law. We do not share your data with any other third party except where required to do so by law or court order, in which case we will notify you unless prohibited from doing so.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active. If you close your account:

  • Your account data (email, password hash, date of birth, entries) will be permanently deleted within 30 days of account closure.
  • Your push notification token will be invalidated and removed immediately upon account closure.
  • Anonymised, aggregated draw statistics that cannot be linked back to you may be retained indefinitely for operational analysis.

We may retain certain records for longer where required by law (for example, financial transaction records required under anti-money-laundering legislation).

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access – to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification – to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure – to request deletion of your data (subject to any legal retention obligations).
  • Right to restriction – to request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability – to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object – to object to processing carried out on the basis of legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent – where processing is based on consent, to withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights please contact us at tom@allunapp.co.uk. We will respond within one calendar month. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe your data has been processed unlawfully.

8. Cookies

The Allun App does not use cookies. As a native mobile application it does not operate within a web browser and therefore no cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies are employed.

9. Children

The App is intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. Date of birth is collected at registration specifically to enforce this requirement. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a person under 18 we will delete that data immediately. If you believe a minor has created an account, please notify us at tom@allunapp.co.uk.
